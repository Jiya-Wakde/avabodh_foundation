About Avabodh Foundation

Avabodh Foundation is a community-driven non-profit dedicated to empowering underprivileged children through accessible education, compassionate support, and organised volunteer efforts. We work to bridge the gap between deprivation and opportunity by creating safe learning spaces, offering mentorship, and guiding children toward formal schooling. With a mission to build an educated and responsible society, we believe in uplifting individuals so they can uplift others, creating a continuous cycle of growth, equality, and collective progress.

Mission

To provide basic education and pathways to formal schooling for street children by offering accessible learning spaces, supportive environments, and inspiring mentorship. We also aim to build a strong movement of organised volunteers dedicated to creating an educated and socially responsible community.

Vision

To create a self-reliant and compassionate society where every individual is empowered with knowledge, responsibility, and equal opportunities, building a cycle of upliftment, inclusion, and sustainable growth.

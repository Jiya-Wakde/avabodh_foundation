About Avabodh Foundation

We teach children in the places they live — street corners, markets, and community spaces. Our goal is to remove barriers that keep children out of school. We provide short lessons, assist families with enrollment, and follow-up support. We also create safe community spaces like the Mind Map Café for people with mental challenges.

Mission

Provide basic education and pathways to formal schooling for street children across our community.

Vision

A world where every child has access to education and a chance to end the cycle of poverty.

Values

Compassion · Respect · Inclusion · Dignity
